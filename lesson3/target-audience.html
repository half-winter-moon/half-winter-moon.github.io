Target Audience

Description

Those who are traveling through Idaho and want to know of the possible experinces available to them. This site can also benefit those of the community who wish to make their goods and services more well known to the public.

Personas

Jemima: I am a local bakery owner that specializes in pastries. I want to reach as broad an audience as I can with my business, including those who are only in town for a short amount of time. I am wanting to make my bakery known and get more customers.

Jared: I am a husband and father of three children. We enjoy family trips and are driving around Idaho and Utah for the weeked. I want to know what there is to see, and where I should stop to give my family a great experience. I would also like to know what the weather will be, and what activities go well with different weather conditions.

Scenarios

  • A family is traveling through town and wants to know what activities or events might be happening that day. They can find them on this page.
  • Someone has recently moved into town and wants to become familier with the popular food places in town. That information is readily available on this page.
  • Someone really enjoys the film "Napoleon Dynamite" and wants to know where the highschool it was filmed at is. This is the site for that!
  • An individual desires to support small business owners in Southern Idaho. They can find out about them on this webpage.
  • A local is trying to decide what to do this weekend and would like to know what activities are recommended for the current weather. This is a convenient location to find that information.
